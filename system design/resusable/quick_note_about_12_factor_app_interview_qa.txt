# Quick Note about 12 Factor App Interview QA

learn about where 12 factor app won't needed and where it is needed.
like 
- 12 factor app is not needed for small apps.
- 12 factor app is needed for large scale apps.

- 12 factor app is not needed for the apps that are not going to be scaled.
- 12 factor app is needed for the apps that are going to be scaled.

- 12 factor app is not needed for the apps that are not going to be maintained.
- 12 factor app is needed for the apps that are going to be maintained.

- 12 factor app is not needed for the apps that are not going to be deployed frequently.
- 12 factor app is needed for the apps that are going to be deployed frequently.

- 12 factor app is not needed for the apps that are not going to be monitored.
- 12 factor app is needed for the apps that are going to be monitored.

etc..
